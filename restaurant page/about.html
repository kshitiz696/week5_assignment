FOOD

Engineer Restaurant is your best destination for great tasting, authentic Indian food in Gwalior, with all your favorite Northern Indian recipes, highlights from the Southern Indian style of cooking, a nd the latest from the exciting fusion style of modern Gwalior.

In fact, we have so many choices on our menu that it is sometimes difficult to decide what to eat! This makes our special-value lunch buffets a tempting option, and an excellent way for you to try a little – or a lot! – of everything.
